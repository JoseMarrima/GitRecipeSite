Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A simple yet delicious pasta dish ready in 20 minutes

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (500g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb (500g) spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 3 minutes.
  2. Add garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook for another 2 minutes until vegetables begin to soften.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low. Cover and cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. While sauce is simmering, cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  8. Drain spaghetti and return to pot.
  9. Serve spaghetti topped with Bolognese sauce, grated Parmesan cheese, and fresh basil leaves.

Chef's Notes

For a richer flavor, you can add a splash of red wine after browning the meat. Let it reduce before adding the other liquids.

This sauce freezes well for up to 3 months. Make a double batch and save half for another meal!
