Garden Salad

Fresh and nutritious salad packed with seasonal vegetables.

Prep Time: 10 minutes Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine all vegetables in a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately for best freshness.
