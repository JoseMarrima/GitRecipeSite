Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist chocolate cake for any occasion.

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour into prepared pans and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans to wire racks.
